SiteFlow

Mobile-first workflow automation for Clinical Research Coordinators

Independent clinical research sites run on fragmented paper trails, sponsor portals, and manual scheduling. SiteFlow is a mobile-first platform built for the ~20,000 CRCs at 9,700 US independent sites — closing the gap Veeva SiteVault deliberately leaves open (sponsor-centric, not site-centric).

CapStack

Mobile-first cross-account scenario planning for active retail investors

Active retail investors (22–35) hold 3+ account types with no unified view for scenario planning. CapStack provides a cross-platform modeling tool that incumbents like Betterment and Wealthfront structurally cannot build — their AUM-based revenue model means they can never go genuinely platform-agnostic.

AI Productivity

DraftDesk

AI writing co-pilot for non-technical knowledge workers — structured, context-aware drafts

Knowledge workers spend 4–6 hours per week on first drafts of QBRs, RFP responses, campaign briefs, and escalation summaries. DraftDesk injects CRM and call-transcript context to produce structured, workflow-ready output. Copilot and Gemini must stay horizontal; Notion AI is a doc tool, not a workflow tool; RFPIO is enterprise-priced — leaving DraftDesk as the only structured workflow tool at per-seat pricing anyone can afford.

70M+ US Knowledge Workers
$18/seat Pro · $14/seat Team
$1.6M ARR @ 7,500 Seats
