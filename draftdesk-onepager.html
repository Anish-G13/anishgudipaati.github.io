Problem
Enterprises have bought ChatGPT Enterprise, Copilot, and Gemini at scale — but abandonment is high. Knowledge workers in CS, marketing, and procurement spend 4–6 hours per week on first drafts that follow the same structure every time. Horizontal AI tools give them a blank prompt box. Most can't use it effectively.
"When I sit down to write a QBR, I want a complete first draft pre-loaded with my account's context — so I can spend my time on strategy, not formatting."
Solution
DraftDesk eliminates the blank prompt state. Workers select a role-specific template (QBR, RFP response, campaign brief, escalation summary), confirm auto-injected context pulled from Salesforce and Gong, and receive a complete, structured first draft in under 60 seconds — not a chat reply, but a reviewable, client-ready document.
